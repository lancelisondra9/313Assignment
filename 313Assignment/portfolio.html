Project 2

Pawfect Match: A dog adoption website made in C# that utilized Bootstrap and the Blazor Web App Funcitonality. The purpose of this website was to help potential adopters find their new home with an easy-to-understand UI and a myriad of different dogs with varying breeds, gender, and age.
